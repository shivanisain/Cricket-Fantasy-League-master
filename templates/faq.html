FAQ

Q What is Fantasy Cricket League?

Fantasy Cricket is a part of the Fantasy Sports genre. It is an online game where you create a virtual team of real cricket players and score points depending on how your chosen players perform in real life matches. To win a tournament, you must work towards attaining the maximum points and the No. 1 rank on the leader-board.


Q What is special about this Fantasy Cricket League?

Using this software you can view all previous data of IPL and be able to take an informed decision on how to select your squad. Please visit the statistics section for more details on what all statistics are being provided in the software. You can also create groups with your friends and be able to create a local leaderboard to see who of you wins. There is also an option of viewing the Global leaderboard to see your overall ranking among all users.


Q How do I select my team?

You should choose the select my squad option and see the list of available players. From there you can add and remove players of your choice till the actual match begins. Based on the players selected you will get points according to the performance of each player.


Q Can I change my Fantasy Cricket team for future rounds?

Yes, you are required to create a new team for every match on Fantasy Cricket League. The players selected must be only from one of the two team playing in that match.


Q How many players are needed to create a team?

 You need to select 10 players to create a team. There is no restriction on selecting any number of bowlers, batsmen or wicket keepers. This leaves you more flexibility to choose you favourite bowlers according to the pitch and weather condition.


Q Can I choose players from one side only?

Yes however we strongly recommend you not doing that to improve your odds of scoring more points!!


Q I’ve spent my entire budget before selecting all 11 players. How do I complete my team?

That’s the challenge! You need to create your team within the budget of 1 million rupees alloted to you.


Q When do you update the squads for rounds on Fantasy Crifcket League?

The squads for subsequent matches shall be updated soon after that days match has ended.
